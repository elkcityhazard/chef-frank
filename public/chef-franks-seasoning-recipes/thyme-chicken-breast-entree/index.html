Thyme Chicken Breast Entree

Thyme chicken breast is prepared with fresh thyme, herbs and spices, then baked in the oven. It makes a good partner to wild rice, or roasted red potatoes, corn bread, salad or fruit.

Thyme Chicken Breast Directions

### Oven Method:
