Meatloaf Recipe For 200 People

Meatloaf for 200 people is a Quick recipe when feeding a crowd. Mashed potatoes and green beans is standard with this meatloaf recipe. Use leftovers for sandwiches or put in spaghetti sauce.

Ingredients

  • 50# ground beef (19/79)
  • 5# diced onions
  • 3# diced celery
  • 50 eggs
  • 3 loaves bread or 3#( or any buns or leftover breads)
  • 3 tbsp beef base
  • 4 c ketchup
  • 1/4 c granulated or chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp white pepper
  • 2 qts water
