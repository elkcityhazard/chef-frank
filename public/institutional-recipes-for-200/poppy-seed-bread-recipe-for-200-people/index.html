Easy quick poppy seed bread recipe for dessert or as a breakfast bread. makes great muffins too. poppy seed bread for 200 will keep your clients tummies happy and is easy to prepare and serve.

Poppy Seed Bread for 200 Ingredients

  • 20# yellow cake mix
  • 8# toasted coconut instant pudding mix (vanilla pudding and add toasted coconut works too.)
  • 20 large eggs
  • 4 c vegetable oil
  • 3 c soaked and drained poppy seeds
  • 6 c hot water

Directions

Mix all ingredients in large mixer bowl, beat as for cake on medium speed for two minutes. Scale out on four greased sheet pans.

Bake in pre-heated oven 350℉ for 30-45 minutes. Test with toothpick.
