Ingredients

  • 11 qts tomato juice
  • 30# ground beef
  • 6 large green peppers diced
  • 4 large spanish onions diced
  • 2 large stalks celery diced
  • 1 c. ground cumin
  • 1 c. chili powder (mild if for kids)
  • 1/4 c. granulated garlic
  • 2 tbs white pepper.

Directions

Place ground beef in pan and cook in convection oven 325‚Ñâ about 1 1/2 hour stirring around every 20 minutes. Place diced vegetables, juice, and spices in kettle and simmer until cooked. Add beans then ground beef(cooked and drained). Let simmer and serve.

Serves 180 to 200 people, how easy is that!
