Scalloped Corn for 200 Ingredients

  • 8 #10 cans cans creamed corn
  • 30 eggs
  • 2 lbs saltines (crushed)
  • 2 lbs butter (melted)
  • 2 c. milk
  • 2 tbs pepper (avoid salt as saltines contain enough)

Directions

In large mixing bowl add corn, 1 1/2 lbs crackers (crushed), eggs, butter, and milk. Mix well. Pour into shallow hotel pans (greased). Put rest of saltines on top. Bake 350â„‰ for 1 hour in convection oven. Remove when firm in pan.

Note: This is an excellent side dish for the holidays and is quite easy to prepare.
