Honey Barbeque Chicken

Chicken Part:

To Cook Inside:
Method #1 In large roasting pan, place chicken and Roast uncovered 375* for 40-60 minutes. When chicken is most of the way cooked, pour Honey Barbeque Sauce over Chicken and place under broiler to finish.

To Barbeque Outside:
Method#2 Place chicken in pot and simmer until chicken is cooked, but not overcooked. Remove chicken and place on preheated barbeque grill. Baste with sauce several times while turning over,  Grillimg both sides until desired doneness is achieved. Serve with salads, rice or fruit




Honey Barbeque Sauce Part:
1 c ketchup
1/2 c honey
1 tbsp yellow mustard
1 tbsp season salt
1/2 onion small diced
1 tbsp garlic, fresh chopped
1/4 c brown sugar
1/8 c vineager
1 tbsp soy sauce
2 tbsp italian herb seasoning
1 tsp ground rosemary
1/2 c red wine
1/8 c molasses

Combine ingredients in sauce pan, simmer for 1/2 hour, stirring frequently. Be careful as sauce wants to splatter. Cover between stirs. Great for outdoor Summer Barbeques. Serve with salad, fruits, or side dishes
