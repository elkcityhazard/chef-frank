Chicken Quesadillas Recipe

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut in strips
  • 1 c salsa
  • 1 pkg fajita seasoning
  • 2 c shredded cheddar
  • 8 12â€³ flour tortillas

Toppings

  • sliced onions and peppers (optional)
  • sliced black olives
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 1/2 c jalapeno peppers
  • 2 c shreeded lettuce
  • 2 c diced tomatoes
  • 2 c refried beans
  • 1 c guacamole

Directions

In large frying pan, brown chicken until cooked then add 2 tbsp salsa and fajita seasoning to taste. In seperate frying pan on medium heat place a flour tortilla, then cheese on top and cooked sliced chicken. Cook until cheese is melted, then fold over or place another tortilla on top and turn over to cook for a couple minutes. Serve with toppings and rest of salsa. Spanish rice goes well with this Mexican recipe.
