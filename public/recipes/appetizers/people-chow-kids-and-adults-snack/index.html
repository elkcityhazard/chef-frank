Quick Easy People Chow Recipe is a favorite among kids and adults alike. People Chow has chocolate and peanut butter goodness. A great Birthday treat!

People Chow Recipe

  • 1 c margerine or butter
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 1 bag chocolate chips
  • 1 box rice chex cereal
  • 1 c powdered sugar

Directions

Mix all ingredients except powdered sugar in a large bowl. Once all is mixed, place in large paper bag with powdered sugar, fold closed and shake vigorously.
