Scratch Mexican Guacamole is a favorite with Nachos, burritos, tacos and quesadillas. Oh, and fajitas too. It’s simple to prepare with fresh avocados, real lemon juice, garlic and spices. If you make too much, it’s easy to freeze until your next Mexican meal.

Mexican Guacamole Recipe

serves 6-10

  • 2 avocados, peeled, mashed
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp minced onion
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • 1/3 c tomato , finely chopped

Directions

Mix all ingredients together, chill. Serve

Note: Goes great with Fajitas, tacos and other Mexican delights
