Mexican Guacamole Recipe

serves 6-10

  • 2 avocados, peeled, mashed
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp minced onion
  • 1 tsp chopped garlic
  • 1/3 c tomato , finely chopped

Directions

Mix all ingredients together, chill. Serve

Note: Goes great with Fajitas, tacos and other Mexican delights
