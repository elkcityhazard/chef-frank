Quick Creamy Scalloped Potatoes, Great Comfort Food Entree on those Cold Winter Days. Warm up the House with a Creamy Scalloped Potato Aroma.

Creamy Scalloped Potatoes

serves 8

  • 3# red potatoes, sliced 1/4‚Ä≥
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 3 ribs celery, diced small
  • 3 c milk
  • 1 c roux
  • 1 tsp chicken base
  • 1 tsp Chef Frank‚Äôs Seasoning
  • 2 oz. butter

Creamy Scalloped Potatoes Directions

Boil potatoes in medium size pot for about 20 minutes. While potatoes are cooking, sautee onions and celery in butter, (adding slights amounts of water when vegetables start running dry). Cook until tender. Then start heating milk for cream sauce, adding seasonings. Stir almost continuous as milk heats, slowly add roux to desired thickness (quite thick). When potatoes are cooked, drain water, then put sauteed vegetables in pot. Pour cream sauce over potatoes and mix. Put in casserole dish and bake at 350‚Ñâ for 1 hour. Serve.

Note: Goes well with baked ham or our special Quick Easy Stuffed Pork Loin Recipe. Apple sauce is a must for this meal as well as a green salad.
