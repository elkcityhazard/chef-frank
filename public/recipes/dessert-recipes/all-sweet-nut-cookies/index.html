Sandy’s allsweet nut cookies are fun and easy to make. A great snack for kids after school or anytime. Get the kids to help make these delicious cookies.

Sandy’s Allsweet Nut Cookies

  • 1/2 c. margerine softened
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 c. chopped pecans
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 7/8 c. sifted flour
  • 2 c. powdered sugar set aside (for rolling cookies after baking)

Allsweet Nut Cookies Directions

Cream margarine and sugar together. Add vanilla, flour, and nuts. form into balls. Place on greased cookie sheet. bake pre-heated oven at 350℉ for about 15 minutes (not brown). Cool, then roll in powdered sugar.
