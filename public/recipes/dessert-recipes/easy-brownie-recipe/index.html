Quick-e-Brownies Ingredients

serves 20

  • 1 1/2 c flour
  • 2 c sugar
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 8 tbsp baking cocoa
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tbsp corn syrup
  • 1 c oil
  • 1/2 c hot water

Quick Brownies Directions

Place in large mixing bowl, salt, sugar, eggs, oil, vanilla, and corn syrup. Mix well. In seperate bowl whisk together flour and cocoa. Put in wet mixture, add hot water and mix. Pour batter into greased 9″x9″ glass pan and bake in preheated oven at 350℉ for 30 minutes. Let cool and serve.
