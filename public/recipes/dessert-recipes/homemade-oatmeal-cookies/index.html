Oatmeal Cookies Recipe

  • 2 sticks butter, softened
  • 3/4 c brown sugar
  • 3/4 c sugar
  • 1 1/2 c flour
  • 3 c quick oats
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla ext.
  • pinch salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 c dried cherries or raisins
  • 1 c chopped nuts (optional)

Oatmeal Cookie Directions

Cream sugar and butter together. Add eggs,vanilla, and salt, mix well. Mix together flour,
baking soda, baking powder, and oats. Add to wet mixture and mix well. Add dried fruit, nuts, mix well. Place desired amount of dough (about one tbsp) on cookie sheet (about 12 per sheet, and makes four sheets). Bake at 375â„‰ for 8-10 minutes. Cool on opened grocery bag. Serve with tall glass of milk.
