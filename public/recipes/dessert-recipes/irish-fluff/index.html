Quick Easy Irish Fluff Recipe for St. Patrick’s Day is made with Marshmallows, Pistachio Pudding, Pineapple, and Whipped Cream. Irish Fluff will top off any St. Patrick’s Day Meal.

Irish Fluff Ingredients

  • 1 pkg instant pistachio pudding mix
  • 1 pkg small marshmallows
  • 1 can crushed pineapple(do not drain)
  • 1 small tub whipped topping

Irish Fluff Directions

In large mixing bowl combine dry pudding mix and pineapple with juice. Mix well,add marshmallows and whipped topping. Mix, then refrigerate for one hour. Serve. This Dessert recipe is great for St. Patrick’s Day Celebrations.
