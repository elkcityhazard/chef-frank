Jubilee Cookie Recipe

Quick Jubilee Cookie Recipe is a light Vanilla Cake Cookie topped with Vanilla and Coconut Frosting. Jubilee Cookies will Quickly become a Family Favorite Treat or Dessert.

Jubilee cookies

About 48 cookies

  • 1/2 c shortening
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 c evaporated milk
  • 2 3/4 c flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla ext.
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 c coconut
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda

Jubilee Cookie Directions

Mix shortening, sugar, and eggs thoroughly. Stir in milk & vanilla. Sift flour, baking soda, and salt, add to ingredients, mix well. Blend in coconut, chill for 1 hour. Place desired amount of dough on cookie sheet (about 12 per sheet). Bake in pre-heated oven 375â„‰ for 8-10 minutes. Cool on opened grocery bag.
