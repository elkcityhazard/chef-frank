Cinnamon French Toast for 4 servings

  • 6 eggs
  • 5 oz. milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon

Directions

Break the eggs into a mixing bowl and blend with whisk. Add the milk, vanilla, cinnamon and whisk briskly. Have a heated and lightly greasedÂ pan or electric skillet at 375â„‰ . Dip your choice of bread into the mixture and place in pan. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until nice and brown on both sides. Serve with butter and syrup.
