Delicious french toast is so simple to make and goes with any breakfast side dish, and served with real maple syrup, its awesome. Use any kind of bread you wish, just use your imagination.

**_French Toast

_** 2 eggs
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp vanilla
Bread (cinnamon, white, whole wheat, etc.)

In mixing bowl scramble the eggs, add milk, and vanilla. Place on pre-heated flat top grill about 375℉. Grill evenly on both sides until golden brown and toast starts to swell. serve with any side  like hash browns, any meat or eggs, or fruit.
