Mexican Cheese Dip with ground beef, is an easy appetizer for dipping crackers, nacho chips, and tortilla chips. This Dip is a great party staple to go with your other appetizers or for an after school snack for the kids.

Mexican Cheese Dip

  • 1 lb soft pasteurized American cheese
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 1 lb ground beef, browned, drained (optional)
  • 1/2 onion diced, sauteed
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1/2 tbsp ground cumin
  • pepper to taste

Mexican Cheese Dip Directions

In large microwaveable bowl, place cheese and milk, heat for 1 minute then stir, heat again and stir add onions and seasonings and ground beef if desired. Stir together and microwave until hot.

Note: Serve with nacho chips, use over nachos, or with Bacon Wrapped Steak.
