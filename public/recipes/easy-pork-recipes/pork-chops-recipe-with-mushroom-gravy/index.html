Pork Chops With Mushroom Gravy Is a Quick Easy Recipe for Anytime of the Week. Brown Pork Chops, then add Mushroom Soup For Gravy. Mashed Potatoes Compliment this Standard Recipe.

Pork Chops with Mushroom Gravy Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 4 pork chops
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/4 onion diced
  • 1 can mushroom
  • 1 pat butter

Pork Chops With Mushroom Gravy Directions

In large fry pan put butter and melt medium high heat. Place pork chops and onions in pan and brown nicely on both sides. Add mushroom soup and mushrooms. Simmer until tender. Serve with mashed potatoes and side dishes.
