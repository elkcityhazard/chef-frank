Grilled Sirloin Steak Recipe

Sirloin Steak Recipe Quick and Easy for the Barbecue Grill, top with Maitre dâ€™ butter while grilling. Serve with any side dish. Great for back yard Barbecues.

Sirloin Steak Ingredients

  • 2 -3 lbs sirloin steak
  • 1 med. onion, thick sliced
  • 1 lb. fresh sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup beer or wine
  • 2 tbs Maitre dâ€™ butter

Directions

Place steak in a hot frying pan and seer for about 30 seconds on each side to seal in the juices.

Then place on barbecue grill with 1/2 of maitre dâ€™ butter on top of steak and cook to desired doneness.

Meanwhile place mushrooms and onions in same pan and fry in 1/2 of maitre dâ€™ butter.

When almost done flash with beer or wine and simmer until steaks are done. Serve with any side dish.
