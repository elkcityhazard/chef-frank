Honey Barbecue Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 c ketchup
  • 1/2 c honey
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp season salt
  • 1/2 onion small diced
  • 1 tbsp garlic, fresh chopped
  • 1/4 c brown sugar
  • 1/8 c vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Italian herb seasoning
  • 1 tsp ground rosemary
  • 1/2 c red wine
  • 1/8 c molasses

Honey Barbecue Sauce Directions

Combine ingredients in sauce pan, simmer for 1/2 hour, stirring frequently. Be careful as sauce wants to splatter. Cover between stirs. Great for outdoor Summer Barbecues. Serve with salad, fruits, or side dishes

To Barbecue Outside
