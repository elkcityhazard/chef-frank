Chicken Quesadillas a Great Quick Easy Mexican Quesadilla Recipe using Fresh Chicken Breasts and Soft Tortillas. Make Fresh Salsa and Guacamole if you Desire from our Quick Easy Mexican Recipes. Quesadillas are Served for Lunch or Supper.

Quick and Easy Chicken Quesadillas Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts cut in strips
  • 1 c salsa
  • 1 pkg fajita seasoning
  • 2 c shredded cheddar
  • 8 12â€³ flour tortillas

Toppings Suggestion

  • sliced onions and peppers (optional)
  • sliced black olives
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 1/2 c jalapeno peppers
  • 2 c shreeded lettuce
  • 2 c diced tomatoes
  • 2 c refried beans
  • 1 c guacamole

Directions

In large frying pan, brown chicken until cooked then add 2 tbsp salsa and fajita seasoning to taste. In separate frying pan on medium heat place a flour tortilla, then cheese on top and cooked sliced chicken. Cook until cheese is melted, then fold over or place another tortilla on top and turn over to cook for a couple minutes. Serve with toppings and rest of salsa. Spanish rice goes well with this Mexican recipe.
