Dottie’s Salmon Patties

Dottie’s Salmon Patties Recipe is Easy to Make and goes with a Spring Salad, Fresh Fruits and Vegetables, or Sandwiches. Dotties Salmon Patties are Wonderful and Versatile, use Your Imagination.

Dottie’s Salmon Patties

Recipe from Dottie F.

Makes 6 Patties

  • 1- 15oz. can salmon
  • 16 saltine crackers
  • 2 eggs
  • some milk ( just enough to moisten)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 onion, diced small (optional)

Directions

Mix altogether, make patties, fry in pan in light oil until golden brown- makes 6 patties.
