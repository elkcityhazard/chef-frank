Karen’s Grape Salad

This Wonderful Salad Made from Fresh Grapes is Great for Potluck Dinners, Holidays, Picnics and Barbecues,or Anytime, and can be Made the Night Before.

Karen’s Grape Salad

Recipe From Karen A.

  • 8 oz. cream cheese
  • 1 c sour cream
  • 1/2 c sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Beat together until smooth
  • Mix with 2 lbs. washed seedless grapes ( red green or both)

Put into dish or 9×13 pan

Sprinkle top with 1 c brown sugar and 1 c chopped pecans

Note: Can be made the night before.

Note: Good dish to take to potluck.
