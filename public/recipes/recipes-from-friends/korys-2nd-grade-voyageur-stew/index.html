Kory’s 2nd Grade Voyageur Stew

This Recipe for Kory’s 2nd Grade Voyageur Stew is Hearty, Easy, and Fun. The Kids Love to Help Make This While Camping or at Home “Chillin” With The Family. Don’t Forget the Dutch Oven!

Kory’s 2nd Grade Voyageur Stew

Recipe from Kathy F.

  • 2 lbs. ground beef
  • 1-2 chopped onions
  • 1 can vegetable soup
  • 1 can cut carrots
  • 1 can corn
  • 1 can peas
  • 1 can green beans
  • 2 cans small potatoes
  • 1 48 oz. red vegetable juice

Directions

Cook meat and onions, drain off fat.

Drain all vegetables.

Mix in all ingredients in kettle and heat through slowly. Stir easily every few minutes.

Yum!! Fits in a Dutch Oven and Easy for Camping!
